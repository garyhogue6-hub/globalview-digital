Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

GlobalView Digital LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our video editing services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with us:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you request a quote or purchase a package.
  • Project Assets: Raw video footage, brand assets, logos, and scripts that you upload or share with us for editing purposes.
  • Automatically Collected Data & Cookies: IP addresses, browser types, and usage data via standard website analytics tools. We use strictly necessary cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website and secure payment gateways.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following business purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and deliver the video editing services you requested.
  • To process payments securely through our trusted third-party processors (e.g., Stripe).
  • To communicate with you regarding project updates, revisions, and customer support.

3. Data Security & Data Retention

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data and raw footage. All project files are stored on secure, encrypted cloud servers. Data Retention Policy: Once a project is finalized and delivered, raw files are kept for a maximum of 30 days before being permanently deleted from our primary servers to protect your privacy and intellectual property, unless otherwise requested.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only with trusted third-party service providers (payment gateways, cloud hosting) necessary to conduct our business.
